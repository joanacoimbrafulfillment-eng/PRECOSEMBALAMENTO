Sales by month (units) — Weighted average consumables

Enter the units sold per product in a month. The system calculates the weighted average consumables cost per unit and the total consumables for the month. Everything is saved (local + Firebase).
Choose the month to fill in.
Weighted average = Σ(units × product_consumables) / Σ(units).
The "consumables" field comes from your product calculation (variables/packaging).
Tip: if you do not fill in a product, it assumes 0 units for that month.
Podes colar em bulk no formato: Name do Product[TAB]Unidades (ou Name;Unidades ou Name,Unidades), 1 por linha.
Bulk paste: names must be exactly the same as in the product list (same text).
